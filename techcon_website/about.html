The Origins and Evolution of TechCon

TechCon began in 2012 as a small developer meetup in Nairobi, Kenya, organized by a passionate group of software engineers and tech enthusiasts. The event aimed to create a space for innovation, collaboration, and sharing of ideas in a rapidly growing tech ecosystem.

Over the years, TechCon has transformed into a global conference attracting thousands of participants from all over the world. From keynote speeches by industry leaders to hands-on coding bootcamps, the event has consistently embraced emerging trends and cutting-edge technologies.

Grace Hopper revolutionized programming by inventing the first compiler and helped develop COBOL, one of the earliest high-level programming languages.

Mathematician & Codebreaker

A key figure in the development of computer science and artificial intelligence, Turing's work in breaking the Enigma code helped end World War II and shaped the digital age.
